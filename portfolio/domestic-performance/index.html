📅 Event Information

Bharatanatyam Performance (Dedicated to Chottanikkara Bhagavathy) • Performer: Dr Shrija Srinivasan (Artist, Researcher, Educator, New Delhi) • Date & Time: Saturday, January 03, 2026, 6:15 PM – 08:00 PM

Venue: Temple Auditorium, Chottanikkara Bhagavathy Temple, Chottanikkara, Kochi, Kerala – 682312 • "We solicit your esteemed presence with Family and Friends."

🎼 Programme Details

1. Mallari

Ragam: Gambheeranattai | Talam: Chaturashrajati Eka
Invocation to Goddess Bhagavathy with rhythmic geometric movements in three speeds.

2. Alarippu

Ragam: Kalyani | Talam: Mishrachapu
Pure dance (nritta); signifies the blossoming of a flower and opening salutation.

3. Shabdam

Ragam: Ragamalika | Talam: Mishrachapu
Depicts a Nayika awaiting Lord Shiva through expressive abhinaya.

4. Varnam

Ragam: Valachi | Talam: Aadi
A complex composition portraying Devyani expressing jealousy and anger toward Valli, beloved of Murugan.

5. Padam

Ragam: Bahudari | Talam: Aadi
Story of child Krishna being punished and Yashoda's loving appeal.

6. Tillana

Ragam: Hindolam | Talam: Aadi
Celebration of music and dance, concluding with Mangalam; ode to Nataraja.
